# Privacy Policy — NotesKeeper

**Last updated: June 20, 2026**

NotesKeeper is a note-taking app: you create notes containing text and photos
(for example, to save the progress of a board game session). **The content you
create stays stored locally on your device** and is not transmitted to our
servers.

This policy describes how the **NotesKeeper** mobile application ("the app",
"we") handles information about you. It applies to the Android app published
under the identifier `com.telen.noteskeeper`.

## 1. Data controller

The data controller is **Telen**, the publisher of the app.
Contact: developer@telen.fr

## 2. Data we process

### 2.1. Content you create (stored only on your device)

When you use the app, you can create **notes** (title, date), **sub-notes**
(name, free text), and **photos** you take with your phone's camera.

This data is saved **exclusively in the app's private storage on your device**.
It is not transmitted to, accessed by, or stored by us or any third party. We
have no access to it. You can delete it at any time from within the app, and
uninstalling the app erases all of this content.

### 2.2. Device permissions

- **Camera (`CAMERA`)**: used only when you take a photo to attach to a sub-note.
  The photo is saved in the app's private storage. The app does not access your
  gallery.
- **Internet access (`INTERNET`)**: used only to send the diagnostic reports
  described below. None of your notes, text, or photos are sent over this
  connection.

### 2.3. Diagnostic and stability data (Firebase Crashlytics)

To improve the reliability of the app, we use **Firebase Crashlytics** (Google).
When a crash occurs, Crashlytics automatically collects technical data: a
Crashlytics installation identifier (randomly generated), crash reports and
associated stack traces, and information about the device and app state at the
time of the incident (model, Android version, app version, available
memory/storage, etc.).

This data **does not contain** the content of your notes or your photos.
Collection is disabled in development builds and enabled in the published
version. The app does not use any advertising or audience-measurement analytics
service.

## 3. Purposes and legal bases (GDPR)

| Data | Purpose | Legal basis (GDPR) |
|---|---|---|
| Content you create (notes, photos) | Provide the app's functionality | Processing performed on your device, with no access by us |
| Diagnostic data (Crashlytics) | Detect and fix crashes | Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) |

## 4. Data retention

- **Content you create**: kept on your device until you delete it or uninstall
  the app.
- **Diagnostic data**: retained by Google per its retention periods (Crashlytics
  reports are generally kept for up to 90 days).

## 5. Recipients and processors

We do not sell or rent your data. The only third party that may process data on
our behalf is **Google Ireland Limited / Google LLC** (Firebase Crashlytics), as
a processor, for diagnostic purposes. See Google's policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 6. Transfers outside the European Union

Google/Firebase services may involve transferring data outside the European
Economic Area, in particular to the United States. These transfers are covered by
the appropriate safeguards required under the GDPR (Standard Contractual Clauses
and Google's compliance mechanisms).

## 7. Security

The content you create is stored in the app's private storage, inaccessible to
other apps under normal Android usage conditions. We recommend protecting your
device (screen lock, encryption).

## 8. Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the rights of access, rectification, erasure,
restriction, objection, and portability.

- Because the **content you create** is local, you exercise these rights directly
  in the app (view, edit, delete) or by uninstalling the app.
- For the **diagnostic data** (Firebase), contact developer@telen.fr. You may
  also lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority (in France, the CNIL —
  www.cnil.fr).

## 9. Children

NotesKeeper is a general-audience utility app. It is not directed to children
under 15, and we do not knowingly collect data about them.

## 10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Any change will be published at this same address,
with an updated revision date.
